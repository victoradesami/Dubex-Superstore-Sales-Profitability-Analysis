Tools: Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts, Dashboard)
Type: Exploratory Data Analysis + Dashboard Design
Dataset: Dubex SUperstore Sales Profitability Analysis


**Project Overview**
The analysis of Dubex Superstore reveals strong sales performance driven by a few high-performing regions, products, and sales personnel. Revenue peaks toward the end of the year, with December emerging as the top-performing month. However, performance disparities across regions, product categories, and sales staff suggest opportunities for optimization and growth.
